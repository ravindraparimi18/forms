Build More Knowledge in Economics For all budding entrepreneurs and economists, knowing the economy and founding basics of economics is highly essential. Gain a good understanding of the Indian economy with books like Indian Economy by Ramesh Singh, Indian Economy: Performance and Policies: 2015-16 by Uma Kapila and others. If world economy interests you, then check out Raghuram Rajan’s Fault Line: How Hidden Fractures Still Threaten the World Economy. Learn titbits of economics from the basic with books like Economics: The Remarkable Story of How the Economy Works by Ben Mathew, Day to Day Economics by Satish Y Deodar and more.
